Youth, the powerhouse of our nation, is crucial in our journey forward. Only by grooming young people who are capable of action, perseverance and creativity, the country can progress with the times. Yuvadhara Magazine is a youth publication that has historically been instrumental in implementing this mission.

Yuvadhara magazine played a major role in uplifting the new generation in the field of art and literature. With the aim of developing the creative and working potential of the youth, Yuvadhara is organizing a Youth Literature Festival at Fort Kochi on May 12, 13 and 14. World famous writers, artists, political leaders and young talent will be part of the festival. A book festival and a food festival are also being organized in connection with this. Yuvadhara Youth Literature Festival, which is going to be a Mega Festival with tens of thousands of participants.
